Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Nexus WhiteHat.

What services does Nexus WhiteHat offer?
We offer web design, web development, custom web applications, Laravel development, full-stack development, React/Next.js development, AI automation, AI integration, server management, Linux administration, cloud infrastructure, DevOps, database administration, and more.
Where is Nexus WhiteHat based?
We are headquartered in Kathmandu, Nepal, and serve clients across 50+ countries worldwide.
How do I get started?
Simply fill out our contact form or message us on WhatsApp. Describe your project requirements, and we'll respond within 24 hours with a custom proposal.
What is your typical project timeline?
Simple websites: 2-4 weeks. Web applications: 1-3 months. Complex enterprise systems: 3-6+ months. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.
Do you offer fixed-price or hourly billing?
We offer both. Most projects use fixed-price billing with clear deliverables. For ongoing work and maintenance, we offer retainer packages.
Do I own the source code?
Yes. Upon project completion and full payment, you receive 100% ownership of all source code, databases, and assets.
What technologies do you use?
We primarily use Next.js, React, TypeScript, Laravel, Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, Docker, Kubernetes, and AWS. We choose the best technology stack for each specific project.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?
Yes, we offer monthly maintenance retainers including security updates, performance monitoring, bug fixes, and feature additions.
Can you work with our existing team?
Absolutely. We frequently work as an extension of in-house teams, providing specialized expertise in areas like DevOps, AI, or backend development.
How do you ensure code quality?
We follow strict coding standards, conduct code reviews, write automated tests, use CI/CD pipelines, and perform security audits on every project.
What is your payment structure?
We typically require 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery. For larger projects, we use milestone-based payments aligned with sprint deliverables.
Do you sign NDAs?
Yes, we are happy to sign non-disclosure agreements before any technical discussions. Client confidentiality is paramount to us.

Still Have Questions?

Our team is ready to answer any questions you have about your specific project.
